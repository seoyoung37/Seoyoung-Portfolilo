Lost and Found

The sky is divided into three vertical panels to express “four seasons in one day” using blendMode(MULTIPLY): a clear blue stack with semi-transparent cloud ellipses; a stormy section in dark blues layered and white wind arcs; and a golden-hour panel with warm bands and a circular sun. A central river and beach is built from quad() and wide rect() forms; small rotate(sin(frameCount*…)) transforms give the water a gentle sway. Lawns are broad, light-green rectangles. On both banks, high-rises, shops, a highway, and a truss-like bridge are assembled from rect(), triangle(), and occasional arc() shapes. Abundant greenery appears as clustered circles in varied greens, while graffiti is hinted through bright, compact rectangles and quads and triangles on walls and the bridge.
