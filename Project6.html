Data Portrait

This data portrait visualizes a week of my walking habits. Rather than simply displaying numerical data such as step counts, it aims to express the rhythm, stability, and energy of my daily walks in a visual form. Each day appears as a small orbit in the composition, where the movements of the left and right feet cross each other, creating unique daily footprints. This visualization captures subtle variations in my physical condition and energy throughout the week. I tracked how much and how rhythmically I walk every day. In addition to step count, distance, and speed, I also recorded step length and double support time, to understand walking not merely as an exercise, but as a reflection of my physical rhythm and emotional state.
