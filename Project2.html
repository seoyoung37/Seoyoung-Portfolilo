Face Generator

I first drew the minimum-scale and maximum-scale faces in Illustrator. Then I planned how the face should change with mouseX and mouseY, and translated those Illustrator coordinates into p5.js. Moving the mouse to the right makes the face wider; moving down makes it taller.
