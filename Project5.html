Optical Illusion

This project aimed to create a visual illusion where circular patterns respond to the movement of the mouse, giving a sense of motion and depth. It began with a simple experiment of vibrating points, but through continuous adjustments to distance, brightness, size, and line length, it evolved into a dynamic pattern that visually ripples with the viewer’s gaze. By using if/else conditions to vary the brightness and length of lines based on proximity, I learned how small parameter changes could significantly affect the rhythm and strength of the illusion.
